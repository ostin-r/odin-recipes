Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

A classic dish with rice, vegetables, chicken, and a blend of spices and coconut milk to make the famous chicken tikka masala sauce

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Tikka Masala Sauce
  • Rice
  • Bell Pepper
  • Broccoli

Steps

  1. Cook Rice
  2. Chop vegetables and cook in skillet
  3. Cook Chicken
  4. Add together chicken, vegetables, and rice in large skillet
  5. Let simmer for 10 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
