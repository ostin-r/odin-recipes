Super Salad

Description

A pleasant summer salad for an energetic afternoon

Ingredients

  • Blueberries
  • Shredded Carrots
  • Kale
  • Peanuts
  • Olive Oil and Balsalmic Vinegar, or your favorite balsalmic dressing
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Add ingredients together
  2. Make dressing and toss
  3. Enjoy!
