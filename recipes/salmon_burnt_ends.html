Salmon Burnt Ends

Description

Salmon glazed in barbecue sauce and smoked until perfection served with hearty green vegetables and brown rice

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Glaze: BBQ sauce, sriracha, soy sauce
  • Rice
  • Hearty Green Vegetable. Suggestions: brussel sprouts or broccoli
  • Green onions for garnish
  • Mayo and sriracha sauce

Steps

  1. Glaze and let salmon sit in fridge overnight
  2. Cook Rice
  3. Cook Vegetables
  4. Smoke fish at 300 F for 45 minutes
  5. Add garnish and sauce
  6. Enjoy!
